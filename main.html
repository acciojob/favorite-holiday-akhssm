Paris


Paris, the capital of France, is renowned for its romantic ambiance, world-class museums, and historic architecture. It’s a dream destination for travelers seeking art, culture, and unforgettable cuisine.

  1. Eiffel Tower – Iconic landmark with breathtaking city views.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous Mona Lisa and countless masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – Stunning example of French Gothic architecture.
  4. Montmartre – Artistic neighborhood with charming streets and Sacré-Cœur Basilica.
  5. Seine River Cruise – Scenic boat ride through the heart of the city.
  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery breakfast pastries.
  • Macarons – Colorful, delicate sweet treats.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Bœuf Bourguignon – Slow-cooked beef stew in red wine sauce.
  • Ratatouille – Classic Provençal vegetable dish.
