Paris, France


Paris, the City of Light, is famous for its art, fashion, cuisine, and romantic atmosphere. From iconic landmarks to charming cafés, it offers an unforgettable experience.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – A breathtaking view of the city.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home of the world-famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – Stunning Gothic architecture.
  4. Montmartre – Artistic neighborhood with lively streets.
  5. Seine River Cruise – Romantic boat ride through the heart of Paris.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Buttery and flaky pastries.
  • Escargot – A traditional French delicacy.
  • Macarons – Colorful and sweet confections.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes with delicious fillings.
  • Ratatouille – Classic vegetable dish full of flavor.
